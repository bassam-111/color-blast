# Privacy Policy for Color Blast

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Overview

Color Blast ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile puzzle game.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

Color Blast is designed to be completely offline and does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Device information
- Location data
- Usage statistics
- Analytics data
- Advertising identifiers
- Any other user data

### Offline Functionality

Color Blast is a fully offline game that:
- Does not require an internet connection to play
- Does not communicate with any external servers
- Does not track user behavior
- Stores game progress locally on your device only

### Local Storage

The app may store game progress (scores, levels) locally on your device. This data:
- Remains on your device only
- Is never transmitted anywhere
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

Color Blast does not use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

Color Blast does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The game is safe for users of all ages as it operates completely offline without any data collection.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
