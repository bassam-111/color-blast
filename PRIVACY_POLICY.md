# Privacy Policy for Color Blast

## Last Updated: January 17, 2026

### 1. Overview
Color Blast ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

### 2. Information We Collect
- **Game Progress**: We store your score and game progress locally on your device
- **Analytics**: We may collect anonymous gameplay statistics to improve the game
- **Device Information**: Basic device information for compatibility purposes

### 3. How We Use Information
- To provide and maintain the game
- To improve game performance and user experience
- To send you technical notices and updates
- To detect and prevent fraud

### 4. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

### 5. Children's Privacy
Color Blast does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

### 7. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@colorblast.com

---
This app does not require internet connectivity and stores all data locally on your device.
